About Me

Reagan Sweat: Art Director
Hello and welcome aboard.
Yes, Sweat is really my last name, and yes, it is pronounced like the classic perspiration. Some people are just blessed, okay?
I thought a lot about how I could compile my person into a few sentences, until I stumbled on this line I wrote in my journal back in Jan 2020:
“If I ever hope to be successful in my career, people need to know that I cared about them, worked hard, and that I shared God’s values in everything I made.”
No matter if it’s Audi or the New York Public Library, each concept is centered around human truths and goodness. I lean more classic than I do trendy and believe that the most eye-catching thing you can do in advertising is bring original concepts and interesting composition to well-known classics.
On the side I’m a photographer, an avid Diet Coke drinker, and a lover of books and indie-folk music (Hello Tyler Childers, Kasey Musgraves, & The Head and the Heart)
Explore and stick around, I’m glad you’re here.
Love, Reagan
